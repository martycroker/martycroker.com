I’m Marty — a remote IT professional, fitness educator, traveller, cookbook creator, and poet. My work tends to sit at the intersection of practicality and reflection: how we move, how we eat, how we organise our days, and how we make sense of it all.

Where I Come From

I’ve spent years working in tech and hospitality while building projects on the side: fitness education, simple cooking systems, travel planning, and writing. A lot of my life has been about balancing responsibility with curiosity — supporting family, staying grounded, and still making space to explore.

What Matters To Me

  • Making things that are genuinely useful in everyday life
  • Keeping systems simple enough that they actually get used
  • Honouring real experience over polished performance
  • Leaving room for play, dogs, and long drives

What This Site Is For

This site is a home base for everything I’m building — a place to share projects as they grow, and to keep a thread running through all the different parts of my work and life.
